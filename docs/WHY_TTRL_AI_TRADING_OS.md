# Why TTRL AI Trading OS Is Different

TTRL AI Trading OS is designed around proof, not prediction hype.

## Evidence First

Every decision must be backed by available data. If evidence is missing, the
system must skip instead of inventing a reason.

## Zero Hallucination Rules

The decision engine rejects unsupported claims:

- no fake whale claims
- no fake news claims
- no assumed market movement
- no guaranteed profit language
- no unsupported BUY/SELL decision

## Conflict Handling

If signals disagree, the system is allowed to `HOLD` or `SKIP`. It does not
force a trade for the sake of activity.

## Full Audit Trail

The backend records:

- market snapshot
- strategy signals
- AI decision
- skipped trade reason
- risk check
- paper intent
- paper fill
- runtime events
- safety events

This makes the system reviewable after the fact.

## Android as Control Panel

The Android app does not contain Binance keys and does not directly place
orders. It shows backend status, decisions, paper trades, audit reports,
license status, and safety controls.

## Public Research, Private Strategy Boundary

The public repository can show safe architecture and interfaces. Production
strategy packs, scoring formulas, signing keys, deployment secrets, and client
license keys should remain private.

## 30-Day Paper Trading Challenge

The most credible next proof is a transparent 30-day paper trading record:

- every decision recorded
- every skipped trade recorded
- every paper fill recorded
- daily paper PnL reported
- drawdown reported
- no deleted losing days
- no profit guarantee language

The public story should be: evidence, discipline, auditability, and safety.
