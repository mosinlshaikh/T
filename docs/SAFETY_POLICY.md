# T Safety Policy



**Project:** T

**Maintainer:** T Technology Research Lab

**Status:** Public Alpha / Research Software



T is designed as a research-only Financial Intelligence Operating System. It is not designed to provide guaranteed profits, investment advice, trading signals, or financial recommendations.



```text

Research only. Not financial advice.

```



## 1. Core Safety Position



T must always be positioned as:



* Research software

* Educational tooling

* Backtesting and analysis infrastructure

* Paper-trading and observation support

* Financial intelligence research system



T must never be positioned as:



* Guaranteed-profit software

* Investment advisory software

* Buy/sell signal service

* Risk-free trading system

* Automated wealth-generation product

* Financial advisor replacement



## 2. Prohibited Claims



The project, documentation, dashboard, CLI output, and generated explanations must avoid phrases such as:



* Guaranteed profit

* 100% accurate

* Sure-shot trade

* Risk-free return

* No-loss strategy

* Must buy

* Must sell

* This will go up

* This will go down

* Financial advice

* Investment advice



## 3. Required Disclaimer



All user-facing research output should include or clearly align with:



```text

Research only. Not financial advice.

```



Where suitable, longer explanations may also include:



```text

This output is for research, testing, and educational analysis only. It should not be treated as financial, investment, legal, tax, or trading advice.

```



## 4. Hallucination-Resistant Output Guard



T includes a hallucination-resistant research guard designed to reduce unsafe financial claims in generated research text.



The guard checks for unsafe language such as:



* Guaranteed profit claims

* Direct buy/sell instructions

* Investment-advice language

* Risk-free claims

* Overconfident market predictions



The guard may sanitize unsafe text and append the required research disclaimer.



## 5. Human Review Requirement



T is public alpha software. Outputs should be reviewed by a human before being used in reports, client communication, research summaries, or technical demonstrations.



No automated output should be treated as final financial advice.



## 6. Backtesting Safety



Backtest results are historical simulations. They do not guarantee future performance.



Backtest reports should be interpreted with caution because results may be affected by:



* Data quality

* Fees and slippage assumptions

* Market regime changes

* Liquidity conditions

* Overfitting

* Survivorship bias

* Incomplete risk modeling



## 7. Dashboard Safety



The dashboard is intended for research visibility only.



Dashboard metrics, equity curves, trade tables, and analytics are not trading recommendations. They are tools for studying system behavior.



## 8. Commercial Safety



Paid services around T may include setup, customization, dashboard configuration, data integration, training, deployment, and support.



Paid services must not be marketed as guaranteed-return investment services.



## 9. Official Safety Statement



```text

T is research-only financial intelligence software.

It does not provide financial advice.

It does not guarantee profit.

It does not remove trading or investment risk.

```
